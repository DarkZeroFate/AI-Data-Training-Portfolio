### Case Study: Translation Evaluation & Post-Editing

**Project Domain:** E-commerce Localization (English to Indonesian)
**Client Profile:** Global E-commerce Platform ("Benjamin")

**Task Description:**
> This project focuses on high-volume translation evaluation and post-editing. The primary objective is not to translate from scratch, but to audit existing translations to ensure they meet strict quality standards. 

**Key Responsibilities & Metrics:**
*   Evaluated and refined pre-existing translated text to ensure grammatical accuracy and natural sentence flow.
*   Verified the correct usage of specific e-commerce terminology and contextual appropriateness.
*   Maintained a consistent evaluation speed of 500 to 1,000 words per hour without sacrificing output quality.
*   Adhered strictly to client guidelines for large-volume data processing.

**Compensation & Efficiency:**
*   Valued at a rate of $0.015 per word.
*   Achieved an equivalent of IDR 262,500 per 1,000 words evaluated (based on an exchange rate of IDR 17,500).
*   Generated a potential hourly output value ranging from IDR 131,250 to IDR 262,500.
